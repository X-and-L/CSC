Categorical

Structures & Constructions

A wonderland for fake mathematicians!

This seminar is devoted to discussing structures in category theory, including higher categories, topoi, homotopy type and universal algebras. At the same time, we also discuss categorical fundations of theories in different mathematical areas, mainly in algebraic geometry and algebraic topology, such as algebraic K-theory, sheaf theory, model categories and homotopy algebra. Number theory, arithmetic geometry, and knot theory are also somtimes covered. The main participants in this seminar are researchers and doctoral students in different fields. You can also have a brief look at the areas you are not familiar with through this seminar.
